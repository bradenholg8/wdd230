Membership Levels & Benefits

NP Membership: Perfect for nonprofits. No fee, but basic listing on our site, event invites, and minimal promotional support.

Bronze Membership: Basic membership includes standard directory listing, access to chamber events, and networking sessions.

Silver Membership: Includes all Bronze benefits plus one sponsored event per year, highlight in monthly newsletter, and discount booth at trade fairs.

Gold Membership: Top-tier membership with priority listings, free booth at trade fairs, social media shout-outs, and exclusive invites to VIP events.
